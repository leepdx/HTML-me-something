Why is my one year old climing the pantry?The answer may surprise you

Patrick has been pushing boundaries since the beginning.

There he is at five days old breaking out of a zipped up swaddle. The swaddle is marketed as being unbreakable by the most Houdini of babies...we considered asking for our money back.

He's never seemed to understand that he's small. He has always been able to express his big personality

At three months old he decided that not being able to walk or crawl would not stop him from chasing the cat...

and he's been trying to pet him ever since!

As he grew his tricks became increasingly impressive.

His first tricks included transforming into various animals...

acting as a wifi hotspot...

eating giant pancakes in one sitting...

and being an award winning Top Gun impersonator.

But the good times came to a halt during his 6 month life crisis.

He had to take a step back and take a good hard look at himself.

He saught cousil from his wise puppy friend, Layla, then all was right in the world of Little Patrick.

Patrick quickly got back on his feet...

and decided to form his own company specializing in wifi hotspots. He spent months networking...

and plannning.

Eventually his hard work paid off and he was able to successfully start his business!

Here he is utilizing his climbing abilities to assess proper instalation of his product.
